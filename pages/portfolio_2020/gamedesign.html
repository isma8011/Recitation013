As a final project for my design class, I was tasked with creating a game in GB Studio. The script and general ideas behind the game were also a product of the design process, as I brainstormed and prototyped potential versions of the game. It is playable through the widget below. (A note to those viewing on laptop: the controls are Enter to start, X and Z to interact, and WASD or arrow keys to move.)
